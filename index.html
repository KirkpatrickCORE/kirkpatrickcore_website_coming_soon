The Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business Program was developed to help agencies meet the government-wide goal that not less than 3% of the total value of all prime contract and subcontract awards be made to small businesses owned and controlled by service-disabled veterans stipulated by the Veterans Entrepreneurship and Small Business Development Act of 1999. Each agency is required by Executive Order 13360 to have a strategy to meet its SDVOSB contracting and subcontracting goals.
